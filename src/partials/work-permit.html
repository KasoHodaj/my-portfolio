Work Eligibility

I was born in Albania and raised in Greece, where I’ve spent most of my life.

I hold a Greek passport, which grants me full rights to live and work anywhere in the European Union without restrictions. I've also completed my mandatory military service in Greece—an experience that taught me discipline, commitment, and teamwork.

Currently, I live in Thessaloniki, Greece, and I’m open to professional opportunities that are full-time, part-time, hybrid, or remote. I'm especially interested in roles where I can continue to grow as a Software Engineer and contribute meaningfully to impactful projects.

If you'd like to connect, collaborate, or learn more, feel free to reach out.
